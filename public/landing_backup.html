Under the hood

The NLP Pipeline

Five carefully orchestrated stages transform raw informal text into clean, formal output — with AI filling the gaps where the dataset can't.

📝
Tokenizer
Splits text into words & phrases, detecting multi-word slang first
🏷️
POS Tagger
Tags parts of speech for context-aware replacement decisions
🔍
Slang Detector
Checks in-memory DB, fuzzy-matches variations, calls Claude AI for unknowns
🔄
Context Engine
Replaces slang preserving surrounding grammar and sentence flow
✅
Formatter
Applies mode (formal / casual / academic) and caches new terms
